Cases (10 Initial):

Intake + differential basics (mixed anxiety/depression)

Goal: rapport, history-taking, screening tools, initial formulation, “what’s the main problem?” prioritization.


Panic attacks / agoraphobia

Goal: identifying triggers, psychoeducation, exposure rationale, safety behaviors, short structured intervention plan.


Major depression with safety-risk screening

Goal: assess severity, function, protective factors, and basic risk assessment + referral workflow (keep it non-graphic).

This is a big “students need practice but placements may limit solo exposure” area.


Trauma-related symptoms (PTSD features)

Goal: pacing, grounding skills, stabilization vs trauma processing, avoiding re-traumatization, consent and boundaries.


Obsessive-compulsive symptoms

Goal: normalize intrusive thoughts, assess compulsions/avoidance, treatment planning (ERP conceptually), motivational barriers.


Bipolar spectrum (hypomania/mania features)

Goal: recognizing elevated mood patterns, sleep changes, risk screening, when to escalate to medical/psychiatric care.


First-episode psychosis / unusual perceptions

Goal: non-confrontational interviewing, assessing reality testing, functional impact, medication/psychiatry referral, family involvement.


Substance use disorder (early stage ambivalence)

Goal: motivational interviewing basics, readiness to change, harm-reduction framing, relapse triggers, stigma-aware language.


Child/adolescent case (ADHD vs anxiety vs learning issues)

Goal: working with parents + school context, consent/assent, developmental history, behavior plans, referral pathways.


Older adult case (cognitive decline + caregiver stress)

Goal: screening for cognition concerns, differentiating depression vs dementia-like symptoms, caregiver support, capacity/ethics basics.
